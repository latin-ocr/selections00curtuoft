33. The march throussh the desert.

[Alexander, after taking Gaza, marched into Egypt, where
he was welcomed as a deliverer. Memphis was surrendered
to him by the Persian governor, and he then determined to
visit the oracle of Jupiter Hammon in the Lybian desert.]
